Your app’s name: Real Estate Asset Manager

Real estate professionals often consider how properties are utilized in order to maximize return on investment. Consider a commercial building - asset managers optimize tenant mix and floor plans. This app aims to assist real estate professionals by giving them a single tool to create, view, update and remove buildings and floor plans.
